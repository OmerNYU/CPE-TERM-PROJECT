User Guide

Please read the guide below to operate the device:

Welcome to MART-B! Your go-to ecosystem after a long day of work to refocus and relax.

  1. Stress Monitor:
    • a. Visual Mode: The lights on the Adafruit will update every 15 seconds to indicate your stress levels. Red - Stressed; Yellow - Tense; Green - Relaxed
    • b. Audio Mode: The device beeps and the frequency of beeping will change to reflect your stress levels. High Frequency - Stressed; Low Frequency - Relaxed
  2. Meditation Assistance:
    • a. Visual Mode: Sync your breathing (inhale-exhale) with the lights on the device
    • b. Audio Mode: Sync your breathing to the beeping. Switch between inhale-exhale when the frequency changes.

To get started, press both buttons on the Adafruit together! Then, follow the instructions on the serial monitor to relax. Happy Relaxing!
